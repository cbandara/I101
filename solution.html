SecureWatch

A watch made for just you

Problem

The probelm that Team Putin set out to solve was one of combatting cyberhacking online through the means of a smart watch. This smart watch has effectively been branded SecureWatch© and will release early 2015 to combat the void in the worlds of both wearable technology and online security.

Solution

This issue of finding a perfect balance between wearable technology and an aesthetically pleasing device was one that required years of thorough research to set out and solve. Team Putin conducted countless hours of testing and certification to arrive at the point of the SecureWatch© today. The SecureWatch© has been crafted to perfectly fill this balance of a smart watch that not only functions as an intelligent device should, but it also blends seamlessly with the lifestyle and appearances of all who may use the device. Also, the SecureWatch© has been created to stand at the forefront of security in authenticating a user when inputting passwords. The SecureWatch© answers the issue of authentication with it's seamless use of Bluetooth technology to help the device function as the ideal physical encrypted authentication tool for all consumers.

Target Market

The SecureWatch© has been created to serve an almost universal market. Due to it's seamless interface and sleek design, users of all ages and purposes will find use in the SecureWatch©. Expectedly, users who are searching for a means of authenticating his or her identity online will find a perfect fit in daily use of the SecureWatch©. The device has been designed to perfectly serve this audience. However, the market for SecureWatch© does not cease at this example, but rather any individual searching for the perfect entry into the world of wearable technology will find exactly what he or she is searching for with the multitude of features built into the SecureWatch©.
